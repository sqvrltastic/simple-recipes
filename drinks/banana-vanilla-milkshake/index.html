Banana & vanilla milkshake

(No image available)

It is exactly what it says in the title. A very tasty and decadent drink that's extremely easy to make. Not for the lactose intolerant!

Ingredients

  • milk
  • vanilla ice cream (make sure you get a quality yellow colored one)
  • banana

Adjust the ingredients in whatever amount you prefer.

Directions

Mix everything in a blender and serve.
