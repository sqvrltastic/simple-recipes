About this website

Simple recipes is a website I made as a hobby project to learn HTML and CSS and also to catalog and organize my favorite recipes I collected over the years.

I used to just dump all of the recipes I came across in my private Discord channel, which I suppose many would relate to, but over time I realized that I want to somehow refine and organize all of them. So I decided to make a website.

Consider this my digital notebook that I mostly made for myself, but decided to share with others because you might find some of it useful for yourself. Note that I do not make any money whatsoever from any of the content posted here. Again, it’s just for organizational purposes.

This website has no ads or trackers and does not need any JavaScript to function. I tried to make it as light on resources as possible. It is built with HUGO static website generator.

Most recipes here are not something super elaborate or fancy nor do I claim the title of master chef or anything like that. Cooking is just my hobby and my goal was primarily to compile a list of recipes that are simple to make and utilize easy to find ingredients. All recipes here are tested by me.

Also, please reach out to me if you spot any typo or grammar mistake or typo or if you have any suggestion on how to improve accessibility and responsiveness of this website! My contact info is listed below.

Some other things to note…

  • I’m European, so all measurements are in grams, meters, Celsius and I utilize European ingredients.
  • I don’t include calorie count or nutritional data because I don’t consider it important for me, sorry.
  • Since I usually cook for more than one person, most recipes yield enough food for two to three people eating.
  • My measuring cup is 200 ml, my teaspoon and tablespoons are… Well standard metal teaspoons and tablespoons I use for eating.
  • I’m a meat eater, so although some recipes are vegetarian, vegan/vegetarian recipes are not something I focus on.
  • This site has an RSS feed. Click here!
  • English is not my first language.
