About this website

Simple Recipes is a website I made as a hobby project to learn HTML and CSS and to organize my favorite recipes I collected over the years.

Like many others, I used to dump all the recipes I came across in my private Discord channel (lol), but over time, I realized that I want to somehow refine and organize all of them. So I decided to make a website! ðŸ’«

Consider this my digital scrapbook that I mostly made for myself, but decided to share with others because you might find some of it useful.

Most recipes here are not something super elaborate or fancy, nor do I claim the title of master chef or anything like that. Cooking is just my hobby and my goal was primarily to compile a list of recipes that are simple to make and utilize easy to find ingredients. All recipes here are tested by me.

With that said, keep in mind that all content here is provided "as is" and I won't guarantee that my tastes are necessarily the same as yours.

This website has no ads or trackers and does not need any JavaScript to function. I tried to make it as light on resources as possible. It is built with HUGO static website generator.

Also, feel free to reach out to me if you spot any typo or grammar mistakes. My contact info is listed below...

"What the hell are buttons?!"

Buttons are small images, most commonly 88 by 31 pixel in size. They can be static or animated. They are a way for webmasters to exchange links in style and build a community of websites. They were really popular in the 90s and early 2000s and they are making a comeback.

If you have a personal website, you can use one of the button images and create an image-link that links to this website.
