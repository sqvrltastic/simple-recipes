Cornbread muffins with chard

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • whole bag of baking powder (10 g)
  • â…“ cup (around 50 ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 cup (200 ml) yogurt
  • 6 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 7 tbsp cornflour
  • 150 g to 200 g any sort of salty salamura cheese*
  • (optional) 200 g to 300 g Balkan blitva (chard)**
  • (optional) kaymak for serving

*I use Pljevaljski cheese that is commonly sold in Montenegro. It has a distinct salty taste and slightly rubbery texture.

**Could be omitted if you do not like it, then you'll just make basic cornbread muffins.

Yields 12 muffins.

Directions

Mix your wet and dry ingredients together until you get a homogenous batter without lumps. Slice your cheese and greens into small pieces and mix them into batter.

Pour your batter into muffin molds and put into a preheated oven on 200 Â°C for 25 to 30 minutes or until you get a golden brown crust. Take out of the oven and molds and let cool slightly. Top with kaymak and serve.
