Greek salad

(No image available)

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • fresh cucumbers
  • black olives
  • red onion
  • feta cheese
  • dried oregano seasoning
  • olive oil (extra virgin is better)
  • any salt with big crystals

Adjust the individual ingredient amounts to your liking, there’s no hard rule.

Directions

Simply chop all of your veggies into small to medium pieces, drop in some olives and small chunks of feta cheese. Sprinkle in some olive oil and season with salt and oregano.
